Privacy policy

How Hana Microelectronics and its affiliates collect, use, disclose and protect personal data — in accordance with Thai law and the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019).

Hana Microelectronics Public Company Limited and its affiliates ("the Company") value your privacy and are committed to protecting personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (2019) ("PDPA"), in effect as of 1 June 2022. This notice explains what data we collect, why, and the rights available to you as a data subject.

Data we collect

Depending on your relationship with Hana — customer, trade partner, job applicant, employee, shareholder, or visitor to our sites — we may collect:

  • Identity & contact data — name, ID/passport number, address, phone, email, signature, photo.
  • Financial data — bank account details, invoices, receipts, delivery notes.
  • Communication data — records of contact with the Company, including recordings where applicable.
  • Employment & professional data — company affiliation, role, education, training and performance records.
  • Technical data — IP address, log files, and information collected via cookies or similar technologies.
  • CCTV recordings — at Company premises for security purposes.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data (such as religion or blood type shown on ID documents) unless legally required, and we request that such fields be concealed wherever possible.

Purposes of processing

We process personal data to operate and coordinate our business, comply with legal and regulatory obligations, maintain premises security, manage customer and supplier relationships, administer employment, and exercise or defend legal claims. Depending on your relationship with Hana, additional purposes may apply — for example, managing a customer order, evaluating a job application, or administering shareholder communications.

Retention period

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this notice — generally no more than 10 years — or for the period required by law, statutory limitation periods, or the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims.

Use of cookies

Our websites use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand how our pages are used, and improve site performance. Information such as browser type or general browsing activity may be collected in aggregate, statistical form. You can control cookies through your browser settings; see our Cookie Policy for details.

Disclosure of personal data

We may disclose personal data to: companies within the Hana Microelectronics Group; government and regulatory authorities where required by law; parties involved in legal proceedings; contractors, auditors and legal advisors performing services on our behalf; certification bodies; and financial institutions. Data is disclosed only as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above.

Transfer to foreign countries

Because Hana operates group companies outside Thailand, personal data may be transferred to other countries, which may have different data protection standards. Any such transfer is carried out in compliance with the PDPA.

Your rights

As a data subject under the PDPA, you have the right to: be notified of data collection; access and request a copy of your data; request data portability; restrict or object to processing; withdraw consent; request erasure; and request correction of inaccurate data. To exercise these rights, contact the relevant facility using the details below.

Security & contact

We maintain administrative, physical and technical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data we hold. For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact the PDPA team at your nearest Hana facility:

  • Lamphun — 101/2 Moo4, Northern Region Industrial Estate, T. Baan-Klang, A. Muang Lamphun 51000 · Tel. (053) 581-567-73
  • Ayutthaya — 100 Moo1, KM.59 Asia Road, T. Bann-Len, A. Bang Pa-in, Ayutthaya 13160 · Tel. (035) 350-803-4

You may also reach us via our contact page.

Amendment of this notice

We review this privacy notice periodically and will post any changes on this page. This policy is effective as of 17 May 2022 and was last reviewed 2 July 2026.
