Frequently asked questions

What is box build?+
Assembling the complete product beyond the circuit board — fitting the PCBA into its enclosure, adding cables/harnesses and mechanical parts, sealing it, and producing a finished, tested unit.
What does Hana’s box build include?+
Enclosure and housing assembly, cable and wire-harness assembly, potting, mechanical assembly and large box build.
Can Hana test the finished product, not just the board?+
Yes — functional test runs at the box-build level, and Hana develops the test rigs in-house, including product-specific rigs.
Can Hana ship finished products directly?+
Yes — finished units are drop-shipped on EXW, DDU or FOB terms.
How is box build different from PCBA?+
PCBA produces the populated circuit board; box build assembles the whole product around it — enclosure, harness, mechanical parts, test and fulfilment.
