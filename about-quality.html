AWARDS

Recognition & Ratings

Independent recognition for corporate governance, ESG performance, and disclosure — from the Stock Exchange of Thailand, the Thai Institute of Directors, the Thaipat Institute, and ASEAN governance bodies.

Corporate Governance Excellence

Hana has consistently received a 5-star “Excellent” rating in the Corporate Governance Report (CGR) from the Thai Institute of Directors Association (IOD) from 2013 to the present — reflecting a continuous commitment to good governance, sustainable growth, and investor confidence.

ASEAN Corporate Governance Excellence

Recognized with the ASEAN Corporate Governance Scorecard (ACGS) Award in the ASEAN Asset Class in 2019, 2021, and 2024, achieving a score of 97.5 or higher — supported by the ASEAN Capital Markets Forum (ACMF).

SET ESG Ratings – Strong Performance

Achieved “AAA,” the top ranking, in the 2025 SET ESG Ratings — following “AA” ratings in 2023 and 2024 and inclusion in the THSI Sustainable Stocks list from 2020 to 2022.

AGM Checklist Excellence

A perfect score of 100 for five consecutive years in the AGM Assessment, reflecting a commitment to transparent and well-governed shareholder meetings.

ESG100 Recognition

Recognized by the Thaipat Institute as one of the Top 100 listed companies for ESG excellence since 2015 (except 2019 and 2025), reflecting the integration of ESG principles across the business.

Sustainability Disclosure Acknowledgement

Received the Sustainability Disclosure Acknowledgement from the Thaipat Institute for five consecutive years, reflecting transparent ESG reporting and advancing UN SDG 12.6.

Thailand’s Top Corporate Brands Values

Recognized as the leading company in Thailand’s electronic component sector for 2023 and 2024, highlighting market position, governance, and sustainable growth.

SET Awards

Honored with the Top Corporate Governance Report Award (2014) and the Best Company Performance & Outstanding CEO Awards (2015), reflecting strong governance, performance, and leadership.
