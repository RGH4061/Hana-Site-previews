2 · The advert

Two or three sentences is plenty. This is the first thing a candidate reads.
Put each responsibility on its own line. The website turns each line into a bullet point.
Same again — one requirement per line.

3 · Sections you don't need to write

These appear on every job page automatically. They're managed centrally so the wording stays consistent across all plants. Nothing to fill in here — this is just so you know what the candidate sees above and below your text.
