Why industrial buyers choose Hana

Built for the long life of industrial electronics

Industrial and IoT products are not consumer volume ramps. They sit in cabinets, plants and outdoor enclosures for a decade or more, and they have to keep working. That changes what a manufacturer has to be good at: ruggedised assembly, disciplined change control over long production runs, and the ability to keep building a product after its first components go end-of-life. We build to that standard, and we run the same qualified processes across more than one country so a program has somewhere to go if it needs to.

01
Ruggedised build

Conformal coating, mixed-technology boards and through-hole for heavy connectors — assembly choices made for heat, vibration and moisture, not the bench.

02
Long-programme support

Change control and obsolescence discipline over runs that last years, so the product you qualify is the product that keeps shipping.

03
More than one country

The same processes run across our sites in Thailand and China — dual-source capacity within one company, not a migration.

What we build

Industrial & IoT products we build

Four areas cover what we build for this market — from the board to the connected product, the tag and the power module inside the equipment.

[ Conformal-coated industrial board ]
PCBA & box build

Ruggedised board assembly through to the tested, finished unit — conformal coating, mixed-technology boards and functional test for controls and equipment that run for decades.

[ Connected device on the line ]
IoT device assembly

Complete connected devices assembled and tested as one unit — board, radio, sensors, battery and enclosure — with wireless functional test before shipment.

FAQ

Industrial and IoT — frequently asked

Common questions from industrial and IoT engineering and procurement teams.

What does Hana build for industrial and IoT?

Ruggedised PCBAs and full box-build products, complete connected IoT devices, RFID asset-tracking tags, and power modules for industrial energy and drives. In most cases we can build the complete unit, not only the board.

Can Hana build products designed to last 10–20 years?

Yes. Long-life industrial programs are the norm here rather than the exception. We apply conformal coating and mixed-technology assembly for harsh environments, and hold change control and obsolescence discipline over long production runs.

Which sites serve industrial programs?

Industrial work runs across our sites in Thailand and China. We treat this as dual-source capacity within one company — a program can qualify at a second site for supply continuity.

Does Hana offer conformal coating and ruggedised finishing?

Conformal coating and harsh-environment finishing are part of our industrial box-build scope. The exact finish set per site is confirmed during the program review.

Start an Industrial electronics build program with Hana

Tell us what the product does and where it runs. We will point you to the right site and processes, and come back with a plan.
