Anti-Corruption

Hana's commitment to operating with integrity and transparency, and our long-standing membership of Thailand's Private Sector Collective Action Coalition Against Corruption (CAC).

Statement from the Chairman & CEO

The Board of Directors recognises that good corporate governance, alongside environmental and social responsibility, is fundamental to sustainable growth for the benefit of all stakeholders. The company is committed to doing business with integrity and transparency through its anti-corruption policy.

Hana has joined Thailand's Private Sector Collective Action Coalition Against Corruption (CAC) to counter corruption in any form. All directors, executives, and employees of the company strictly conform to the anti-corruption policy — and we expect the same of our stakeholders, in support of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Hana Microelectronics Board of Directors
