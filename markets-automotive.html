The Hana automotive advantage

Automotive buyers measure a supplier on quality discipline, traceability and resilience. Here is where Hana stands.

Automotive quality system

IATF 16949 lines with PPAP and APQP discipline — the documentation and process control automotive programs require, not bolted on after the fact.

Traceable by default

Material, unit and product traceability captured at every step, so a field question can be traced back to a board, a reel and a shift.

Resilient footprint

The same processes are qualified across China, Thailand, Cambodia and the USA — so a program can shift sites for capacity or supply continuity.

Relationships that last

Some automotive programs have run with Hana for over twenty years. Continuity of people and process is part of the quality story.
