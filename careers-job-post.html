Pre-Assembly Engineer

Ayutthaya, Thailand Full Time Engineering Posted 27 Jan 2026
Working at Hana

Join a team that builds what the world runs on

At Hana Group, we deliver high-performing manufacturing solutions that solve real challenges for customers across the globe. Behind that mission are the people who drive our processes, develop our strategies, and show up for our customers every day.

When you join Hana, you join a genuinely multinational team working alongside leading OEM companies across Asia and beyond. We value what you bring today and invest in where you're headed — your development matters to us, not just the output.

Our people are the foundation of everything we achieve. If you share our drive to pursue excellence and take pride in your work, you'll find your place here.

About the role

What you'll be doing

As a Pre-Assembly Engineer at our Ayutthaya facility, you will be responsible for the accuracy and readiness of assembly documentation before production begins. You will work closely with customers, production planning, and the engineering team to ensure a smooth transition from order to production floor.

Responsibilities

Key responsibilities

  • Review customer assembly documents and provide guidance on Assembly Instructions, Build Sheets, and Marking Instructions
  • Translate or guide the translation of customer documents into required internal formats
  • Ensure correctness of all information per the PC loading schedule
Requirements

What we're looking for

  • Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Electronics, Electrical, Mechanical, or a related field
  • Newly graduated candidates are welcome; or at least 3 years of work experience in electronics manufacturing
  • Good English communication skills — written and spoken
  • Computer proficiency in AutoCAD and Microsoft Office
What we offer

  • Health insurance and welfare benefits
  • Annual leave plus Thai public holidays
  • Technical training and career development programmes
  • On-site facilities including canteen and transport support
  • A close-knit team culture with strong focus on employee wellbeing
